Urban Policy & Educational Equity

The Problem: California’s century-old "categorical" funding system was rigid, lacked transparency, and failed to adequately support disadvantaged student populations.

The Solution: An exhaustive analysis of the Local Control Funding Formula (LCFF) and high-stakes legislative mandates (AB 97/SB 91) that shifted power to local districts to prioritize equity-based resource allocation.

The Impact: Evaluating the efficacy of site-level interventions, including the Equity Multiplier and Community Schools model, to ensure funding translates into measurable outcomes for English learners and foster youth.
